Albert Einstein

German-born theoretical physicist who is widely held to be one of the greatest and most influential scientists of all time.

  • 1879Born in Ulm, Württemberg, Germany
  • 1897At the age of seventeen, he enrolled in the mathematics and physics teaching diploma program at the Swiss federal polytechnic school in Zürich
  • 1901Acquired Swiss citizenship, which he kept for the rest of his life.
  • 1903Secured a permanent position at the Swiss Patent Office in Bern
  • 1905Submitted a successful PhD dissertation to the University of Zurich. These years are described as his annus mirabilis (miracle year), Einstein published four groundbreaking papers
  • 1914Moved to Berlin in order to join the Prussian Academy of Sciences and the Humboldt University of Berlin.
  • 1917Became director of the Kaiser Wilhelm Institute for Physics
  • 1955Died at the age of 76, Einstein refused surgery, saying, "I want to go when I want. It is tasteless to prolong life artificially. I have done my share; it is time to go. I will do it elegantly."
